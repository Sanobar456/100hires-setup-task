## Talk 1 — The Brave New World of SEO (SEO Week 2025)

**Source:** https://ipullrank.com/seo-week-2025-mike-king

### Summary

In his SEO Week 2025 keynote, Mike King explains why traditional SEO is no longer enough in an AI-first search environment. He introduces the concept of **Relevance Engineering**, which combines information retrieval, AI, content strategy, UX, and digital PR to improve how content is discovered across search engines and AI assistants.

He explains that AI systems rely on semantic similarity rather than simple keyword matching. His research found that content with strong topical relevance, authoritative sources, and supporting statistics is more likely to appear in AI-generated answers. Mike also demonstrated a RAG-based content editor that analyzes how well content matches AI retrieval systems and provides recommendations for improving AI visibility.

### Why it matters

This talk shows that AI search is changing how content is evaluated. Instead of focusing only on rankings, marketers should create content that AI systems can easily retrieve, understand, and use when generating answers.

## Talk 2 — SEO Week 2026: AI Infrastructure & Retrieval Speed

**Source:** https://www.linkedin.com/showcase/seo-week/

### Summary

Mike discusses how technical performance has become an important factor for AI-powered search. He explains that AI assistants must successfully retrieve a page before they can use it in a response. Slow loading pages, server timeouts, and blocked AI crawlers can prevent content from being considered during retrieval.

He also introduces the idea of **Machine Media**, where AI agents increasingly act as the primary gateway between users and online content. In this environment, technical SEO, infrastructure, and content quality all work together to improve AI visibility.

### Why it matters

This session highlights that technical SEO now goes beyond traditional search engines. Website performance, crawlability, and infrastructure play an increasingly important role in helping AI systems access and recommend content.
