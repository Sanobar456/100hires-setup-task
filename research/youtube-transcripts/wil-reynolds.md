## Talk 1 — The Other AI That Is Hurting Your Organic Traffic (SEO Week 2025)

**Theme:** Why business outcomes matter more than search traffic in the AI era

### Key Insights

- Wil argues that marketers should think beyond traditional SEO because people increasingly expect answers instead of clicking through search results.
- Seer's own experience showed that organic traffic declined significantly while revenue and qualified leads continued to increase.
- He believes business success should be measured using pipeline, revenue, and customer impact rather than traffic alone.
- Authentic engagement is more valuable than artificially boosting content reach through influencer amplification.
- AI should support research and audience understanding, while humans remain responsible for expertise, perspective, and final content creation.

## Talk 2 — Trust, AI Visibility & What Actually Moves Pipeline (SEO Week 2026)

**Theme:** Building trust instead of chasing AI visibility metrics

### Key Insights

- Wil warns that publishing large volumes of AI-generated content without original expertise can weaken brand credibility.
- High-quality content should demonstrate real experience instead of attempting to manipulate AI search systems.
- Direct traffic, referrals, newsletters, private communities, and word-of-mouth continue to generate stronger business outcomes than many traditional SEO metrics.
- He recommends regularly reviewing how AI platforms describe your company and compare it with competitors.
- Seer developed internal AI workflows that interview subject matter experts and convert those conversations into structured content drafts while keeping humans involved throughout the process.

## Webinar — Building an AI-Fluent Team

**Theme:** Creating an organization that effectively uses AI

### Key Insights

- Seer encourages employees to experiment with AI by sharing automation ideas that anyone in the company can build and test.
- Internal experimentation has led to dozens of AI solutions created by employees across different teams.
- Research at Seer shows that many ChatGPT users include brand names directly in their prompts, making brand awareness increasingly important in AI search.
- Wil recommends analyzing AI search behaviour separately by industry because adoption and traffic patterns differ significantly across sectors.
- His overall message is that AI should improve how teams work and learn rather than replace human expertise.
