# Video / Episode 1

## Traditional SEO vs AI Search Optimization

**Source:** https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/crawling-mondays-videos/

**Supporting Transcript:** https://www.advancedwebranking.com/blog/adapting-old-seo-rules-to-the-new-ai-search

### Key Takeaways

#### AI search builds on good SEO practices

Aleyda explains that many SEO best practices still apply in AI search. Creating helpful, well-structured content that satisfies user intent remains the foundation for earning visibility.

---

#### Structure matters more than ever

Since AI systems combine information from multiple sources, content should be organized with clear headings, logical sections, and concise answers that are easy to understand.

---

#### Brand mentions build authority

In addition to backlinks, mentions across platforms like Reddit, newsletters, podcasts, and social media help strengthen a brand's visibility and credibility in AI search.

---

## Why this source matters

This discussion shows that AI search is an evolution of SEO rather than a complete replacement, making it valuable for understanding long-term optimization strategies.

# Video / Episode 2

## Google AI Mode vs Traditional Search

**Source:** https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/crawling-mondays-videos/

### Key Takeaways

* AI crawlers interact with websites differently than traditional search engines, making technical accessibility increasingly important.
* Users now discover information across Google, AI assistants, social platforms, and short-form video, creating a multi-platform search journey.
* AI search performance should be measured by visibility, citations, and brand presence instead of only clicks and rankings.
* Topic clusters and content that supports every stage of the customer journey remain an effective SEO strategy.

---

## Why this source matters

The episode explains how search behavior is changing and why businesses need to optimize for multiple discovery channels instead of relying only on traditional search engines.

# Video / Episode 3

## Preparing for the Agentic Web

**Source:** https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/crawling-mondays-videos/

### Key Takeaways

Aleyda predicts that AI agents will increasingly perform tasks such as researching products, making purchases, and interacting with websites on behalf of users. She encourages businesses to prepare their websites and content for this shift by improving accessibility, structured data, and machine-readable information.

---

## Why this source matters

This discussion introduces the concept of the agentic web and highlights why marketers should begin preparing for AI-driven interactions beyond traditional search.
