# Episode 1

## Google AI Mode — What It Means for Search, Marketing & SEO

**Published:** May 2025

**Source:** https://metacast.app/podcast/the-ross-simmonds-show/LySBYid9

### Key Takeaways

* Google AI Mode is changing how users discover information by providing complete AI-generated answers instead of only traditional search results.
* Marketers should focus on understanding search intent and creating content that answers real user questions rather than optimizing only for keywords.
* Content distribution is becoming more important because AI systems gather information from trusted sources across the web, not just company websites.
* AI-powered features and agentic workflows are creating new opportunities for businesses to automate customer journeys and lead generation.

### Why this source matters

This episode explains how AI search is changing both SEO and content marketing, highlighting the importance of creating useful content and distributing it across multiple trusted platforms.

# Episode 2

## The State of Search in 2026

**Guest:** Garrett Sussman

**Source:** https://podcasts.apple.com/podcast/create-like-the-greats/id1653650073

### Key Takeaways

* Search has expanded beyond Google, with users discovering information through Reddit, YouTube, ChatGPT, Perplexity, TikTok, and other platforms.
* Reddit and YouTube have become valuable sources for both users and AI systems, making community participation and video content increasingly important.
* High-quality content, storytelling, and understanding user intent remain effective regardless of how search platforms evolve.
* Personalization and AI memory are expected to play a much bigger role in search experiences over the next few years.

### Why this source matters

The discussion shows that modern SEO is no longer limited to search engines. Brands need to build visibility across multiple platforms while continuing to create valuable, trustworthy content.
