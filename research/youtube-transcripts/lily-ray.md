# Video / Talk 1

## The Vicious Cycle of SEO: How We Got Here & Where We're Going

**Event:** SEO Week 2025

**Source:** https://ipullrank.com/seo-week-2025-lily-ray

### Key Takeaways

* Google increasingly evaluates websites at the domain level rather than judging individual pages in isolation.
* Websites demonstrating real expertise, authentic user experiences, and strong brand authority continue to perform well after major algorithm updates.
* Many websites that relied on large-scale AI-generated or low-value content experienced significant ranking losses following Google's Helpful Content and Core Updates.
* Lily highlights that Google repeatedly adapts its algorithms to reduce the effectiveness of manipulative SEO tactics, making long-term quality more valuable than short-term shortcuts.
* Building a recognizable personal or company brand contributes to stronger trust signals and greater visibility across search.

### Why this source matters

This presentation explains why sustainable SEO depends on expertise, credibility, and long-term value instead of scalable content tactics that may stop working after future algorithm updates.

# Video / Talk 2

## What the SEO Industry Is Getting Wrong About AI Search

**Published:** May 2026

**Source:** https://ppc.land/lily-ray-what-the-seo-industry-is-getting-dangerously-wrong-about-ai-search/

### Key Takeaways

* AI search optimization should focus on creating genuinely helpful content rather than publishing pages designed only to influence AI-generated answers.
* Google and Microsoft continue improving their ability to identify spam, manipulative comparison pages, and other low-quality AI content.
* Technical SEO remains essential, including allowing AI crawlers to access important content, ensuring JavaScript renders correctly, and implementing structured data where appropriate.
* Businesses should prepare for AI-driven search by strengthening technical foundations instead of relying on temporary optimization tactics.

### Why this source matters

The discussion reinforces that successful AI search optimization combines technical SEO, trustworthy content, and a strong brand rather than relying on shortcuts or AI-generated content at scale.
