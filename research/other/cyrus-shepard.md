## Key Research & Resources

### AI Citation Ranking Factors (2026)

**Source:** https://signal.zyppy.com/p/ai-citation-ranking-factors

This research combines findings from dozens of industry studies, Google patents, and experiments to identify the factors that most influence AI citations.

The biggest takeaway is that traditional SEO remains the foundation for AI visibility. Strong rankings, accessible content, topical authority, and clear answers consistently improve the chances of being referenced by AI systems.

One notable conclusion is that there is currently very little evidence showing that an **LLMs.txt** file directly improves AI citations.

### AI Citation Audit Framework (2026)

**Source:** https://signal.zyppy.com/p/ai-citations-framework

### Google Click Signals & AI Answers (2026)

**Source:** https://signal.zyppy.com/p/google-click-signals

Cyrus explains how user engagement signals influence both traditional search rankings and AI-generated answers.

His research suggests that satisfying search intent quickly and providing a better user experience can increase the likelihood of appearing in AI-generated responses.

---

### Google Winners vs. Losers Study (2024)

**Source:** Zyppy Blog & LinkedIn

Through a large content analysis, Cyrus identified common traits shared by higher-performing websites.

The strongest patterns included genuine first-hand experience, fewer intrusive ads, better navigation, and higher overall content quality.

The research reinforces that experience-driven content consistently outperforms pages created mainly for search engines.

The scorecard is useful for improving content quality before publication rather than relying only on rankings after publishing.

## Main Research Themes

Across his recent work, several themes appear consistently:

* Traditional SEO continues to support AI visibility.
* User experience and click satisfaction remain important.
* Strong brands are cited more frequently than weak brands.
* Helpful, experience-based content performs better than generic AI-generated content.
* AI optimization should build on proven SEO fundamentals instead of replacing them.
