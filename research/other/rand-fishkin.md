## Rand Fishkin — Supporting Resources

### SparkToro

Website: https://sparktoro.com

SparkToro is Rand Fishkin's audience research platform. Instead of focusing only on keywords, it helps marketers discover where their target audience spends time online, including YouTube channels, podcasts, Reddit communities, websites, newsletters, and social media accounts. This makes it useful for planning content distribution in an AI-first search environment.

---

### SparkToro Blog

https://sparktoro.com/blog

Rand regularly publishes articles based on search behavior, audience research, zero-click searches, AI visibility, and modern marketing trends. Many of his posts include original data and practical recommendations for marketers adapting to AI-powered search.

---

### Zero Click Marketing (Book)

Rand co-authored *Zero Click Marketing* with Amanda Natividad. The book explains why businesses should focus less on generating website traffic and more on building brand awareness across multiple platforms. It provides practical strategies for succeeding in a world where users increasingly get answers directly from AI assistants and search engines.

---

### Lost and Founder (Book)

*Lost and Founder* shares Rand's experience building Moz and discusses lessons about entrepreneurship, marketing, company growth, and leadership. Although it is not AI-focused, it provides useful background on his approach to marketing and business.

---

### Key Research Topics

Across his articles, talks, and SparkToro research, Rand frequently focuses on:

* Zero-click searches and their impact on website traffic
* Audience-first marketing strategies
* Brand visibility across AI search platforms
* Measuring marketing beyond clicks and rankings
* Content distribution based on audience behavior
* Long-term brand building instead of traffic-focused SEO
