## Key Themes Across His Work

After reviewing Kevin Indig's recent articles and research, I noticed a few recurring themes:

* AI search is changing SEO from a traffic-focused channel to a visibility-focused channel.
* Brand authority and trust are becoming more important for AI-generated search results.
* Short, focused content often performs better than long "ultimate guide" articles.
* SEO is becoming more connected with content marketing, PR, social media, Reddit, and YouTube.
* Success should be measured through visibility, AI citations, and business impact rather than rankings alone.
