## Key Articles & Research

### Machine Media: The Death of the Open Web (June 2026)

**Source:** https://ipullrank.com/machine-media

**Summary**

Mike King explains how AI agents are becoming the primary way people discover information online. Instead of browsing websites directly, users increasingly rely on AI assistants to retrieve and summarize content. He argues that brands need to make their content accessible to AI systems or risk becoming invisible in AI-generated search experiences.

**Why it matters**

This article introduces the concept of "Machine Media" and explains why optimizing for AI retrieval is becoming just as important as optimizing for traditional search engines.

---

### How AI Mode Works and How SEO Can Prepare (August 2025)

**Source:** https://ipullrank.com/how-ai-mode-works

**Summary**

This article provides a detailed explanation of Google's AI Mode and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Mike explains how AI retrieves, evaluates, and combines information from multiple sources before generating answers. He also discusses how semantic relevance, embeddings, and passage-level optimization influence AI visibility.

**Why it matters**

It provides one of the most detailed technical explanations of how AI-powered search works and offers practical guidance for creating content that AI systems can retrieve more effectively.

---

### SEO Week 2025 Keynote — The Brave New World of SEO

**Source:** https://ipullrank.com/seo-week-2025-mike-king

**Summary**

Mike introduces the concept of Relevance Engineering, which combines AI, information retrieval, UX, content strategy, and digital PR into a broader approach to search optimization. He also demonstrates a RAG-based content editor designed to improve content for AI search.

**Why it matters**

The keynote shows how SEO is expanding beyond keyword optimization and becoming more focused on helping AI systems understand and retrieve content.
